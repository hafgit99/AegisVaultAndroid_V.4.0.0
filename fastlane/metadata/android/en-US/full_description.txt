Aegis Vault is an open source password manager focused on local-first privacy and strong device security.

Your vault stays on your phone by default. Data is encrypted at rest using SQLCipher (AES-256-GCM) with keys derived from your credentials, and can be protected with biometrics.

Key features:
- Local encrypted vault with no ads and no trackers
- Password entries with attachments and categories
- Built-in TOTP for two-factor authentication
- Breach check integration via Have I Been Pwned
- Backup and restore tools for safe migration
- Autofill support for compatible Android apps
