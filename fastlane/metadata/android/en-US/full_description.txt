Aegis Vault is an offline, privacy-first password and authenticator (TOTP) manager designed with top-tier security. It relies on AES-256-GCM encryption to safely store all your credentials locally on your device without transmitting anything over the internet.

Features:
- Completely offline and open-source for absolute privacy.
- Industry-standard AES-256-GCM encryption.
- Built-in Authenticator (TOTP) functionality.
- Biometric login support (Fingerprint/Face).
- Autofill service for seamless logins across your apps and browser.
- Encrypted export and import functionality.

Take control of your digital identity with Aegis Vault.
